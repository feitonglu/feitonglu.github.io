GoldenGate Kids is a specialized business dedicated to treating children with Tethered Oral Ties (TOTs). From initial consultation to post-operative care, we are committed to providing the highest level of care and helping children overcome the challenges of TOTs. Contact us today to learn more about our personalized treatment plans.

TOT Issues

  • Affects speech development
  • Causes eating and breathing difficulties
  • Leads to jaw discomfort and misalignment
  • Can result in dental problems and improper mouth development
  • May cause sleep apnea
